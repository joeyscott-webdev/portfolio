ABOUT ME

My name is Joey Scott and I am a Front-end Developer.
As a teen I developed a love for software and web development. I sidelined those ambitions to join the Army in 2005. While in, I found myself constantly working on things like the organizations websites or databases because I was always drawn to the creative process of development.
In 2018 I decided to pursue that passion.
